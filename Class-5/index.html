BASIS Executive Councils'(2022-2023)

BASIS members have elected a new 11-member Board of Directors known as Executive Council (EC) for a two years term by direct voting on December 26, 2021. The EC has the overall responsibility for running the affairs of BASIS and setting policy guidelines for its secretariat.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To develop the ambition, capacity and sustainable growth of the BASIS
member companies, and to lead and deliver the BASIS contribution to
Smart Bangladesh.

Vision

To lead the development of a vibrant, sustainable and successful
software and IT service industry in Bangladesh.
